# Code of Conduct

Sophia SEO Suite is a SophiaXT project. Everyone working in this repository —
team members, collaborators, and AI agents — is expected to act professionally
and in good faith.

## Standards

- Be respectful, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent; disagree on substance, not people.
- Prioritize user safety and security in every decision (see [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md)).
- Do not build or enable abusive SEO tactics — spam, fake rankings/indexing,
  auto-comment bots, or ranking guarantees are out of scope by policy.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces (repo, issues, PRs, reviews) and to anyone acting
on the project's behalf.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to **conduct@sophiaxt.com**. Maintainers may remove, edit, or
reject contributions and access that violate this code.
